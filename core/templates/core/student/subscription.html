Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free plan work?

The free plan gives you lifetime access to 2 exam boards and 2 different quizzes per topic. You can retake the same quizzes unlimited times to practice!

What happens when I upgrade to PRO?

You'll immediately get access to unlimited quizzes, up to 5 exam boards, and all PRO-exclusive content. Your payment is secure through PayFast.

Can I cancel my PRO subscription?

Yes! You can cancel anytime from this page. You'll continue to have PRO access until the end of your current billing period, then automatically downgrade to Free.

Is my payment secure?

Absolutely! We use PayFast, South Africa's leading payment gateway, with industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information.

What if I have more than 2 exam boards and downgrade?

Your existing selections will be kept, but you'll need to update them to 2 or fewer boards. We'll send you a reminder email.

Cancel PRO Subscription?

Are you sure you want to cancel your PRO subscription and downgrade to Free?

You will lose:

  • Unlimited quizzes
  • Access to 5 exam boards
  • PRO-exclusive content
  • Priority support
