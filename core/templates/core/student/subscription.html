Frequently Asked Questions

How does per-subject pricing work?

For 1-3 subjects, you pay R{% if pricing.per_subject %}{{ pricing.per_subject.price|floatformat:0 }}{% else %}100{% endif %} per subject. For example, 2 subjects would cost R{% if pricing.per_subject %}{{ pricing.per_subject.price|floatformat:0|add:pricing.per_subject.price|floatformat:0 }}{% else %}200{% endif %}/month.

When should I choose the bundle?

If you need 4+ subjects, the bundle plans offer significant savings compared to individual pricing.

Can I change my plan?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing date.

What's included in tutor support?

The R{% if pricing.tutor_addon %}{{ pricing.tutor_addon.price|floatformat:0 }}{% else %}500{% endif %} add-on gives you access to qualified tutors for live sessions and homework help.

Cancel Subscription?

Are you sure you want to cancel? You'll lose access to premium features at the end of your billing period.
