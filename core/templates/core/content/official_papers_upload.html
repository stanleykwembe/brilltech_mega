Folder Structure Guide

Organize your files in the following structure:

SUBJECT_CODE (e.g., 0580, 9MA0)
YEAR (e.g., 2023, 2024)
filename.pdf (e.g., 0580_s23_qp_21.pdf)
filename.pdf (e.g., June 2023 Paper 1.pdf)

Filename Parser supports:

  • Sessions: June, November, May, Summer, Winter (detected automatically)
  • Paper Types: QP (Question Paper), MS (Marking Scheme), ER (Examiner Report)
  • Paper Numbers: 1, 2, 3, 4H, 1F (extracted from filename)
  • Variants: v1, v2, v3 (for Cambridge-style papers)
