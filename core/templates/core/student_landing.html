Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Is EduTech really free forever?

Yes! Our Free plan gives you access to 2 exam boards, unlimited notes and flashcards, 2 different quizzes per topic (with unlimited retries), and free exam paper downloads. No credit card required, ever.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Pro gives you unlimited quizzes (instead of 2 per topic), support for 5 exam boards (instead of 2), priority support, and early access to new features. Free users can still retry quizzes unlimited times!

Which exam boards do you support?

We support Cambridge (IGCSE/A-Level), Edexcel, CAPS (South Africa), ZIMSEC (Zimbabwe), IEB (South Africa), AQA, and OCR. All content is aligned with official syllabuses!

How do the quizzes work?

Our interactive quizzes include multiple choice, true/false, fill-in-the-blank, matching, and essay questions. You get instant feedback on your answers and can track your progress over time. Free users can take 2 different quizzes per topic but retry them unlimited times!

Can I study offline?

Yes! You can download notes and exam papers to study offline. Your quiz progress syncs automatically when you're back online.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Simply click "Upgrade to Pro" from your dashboard and complete the secure PayFast payment (R100/month). Your Pro features activate immediately, and you can cancel anytime!
